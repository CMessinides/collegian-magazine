Based on the Kenyon College campus in the town of Gambier, Ohio, the *Collegian Magazine* publishes thoughtful student journalism that reaches far beyond the bounds of our little hamlet. Our writers tackle issues that range from Ohio's education funding to the assassination of former Swedish Prime Minister Olof Palme.
